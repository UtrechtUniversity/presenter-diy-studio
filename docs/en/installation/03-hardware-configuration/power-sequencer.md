Remove the plate marked “F”. Using a flat-head screwdriver, turn the potentiometer beside *DLY ADJ* to the **left**, until it points between the 8 and 9 o'clock positions. This reduces the interval between switching the three groups on and off from the default 30 seconds to approximately 5 seconds.
