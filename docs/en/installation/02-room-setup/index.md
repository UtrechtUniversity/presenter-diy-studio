The minimum dimensions for a suitable room are:

2.80 m wide, 5.00 m deep and 2.40 m high.

- If the room is between 2.80 m and 3.50 m wide, rounded corners must be created on the left and right between the rear and side walls.
- If the room is between 2.40 m and 2.60 m high, the top of the green screen must curve onto the ceiling and continue for approximately 1.20 m.
- A room cannot be used if it is both less than 3.50 m wide and less than 2.60 m high.
