1. Reserve a studio using the button on the relevant studio page on www.uu.nl. You will receive the necessary information, tips and a tutorial video by email.

2. Go to the library desk and ask for the DIY Studio access card. Sign in and collect the visitor card used to open and lock the studio.

3. On entering the room, you will immediately see a large instruction poster. To the right of the entrance is an ON/OFF wall switch box, clearly marked by a large yellow arrow, with a key in it. Turn the key a quarter-turn towards ON to switch on all studio equipment except the PC.

4. Go to the workstation and switch on the computer using the button attached to the desktop.

5. Once the computer has started, sign in with your Solis ID. You can then watch an instructional video.

6. Complete all steps in the application on the computer and make your recordings.

7. End your session in the application by opening the **Shut down** tab and clicking **Shut down**. The computer will shut down automatically.

8. Turn the key beside the entrance back by a quarter-turn. All lights and other equipment will switch off. Lock the room with the visitor card and return it to the desk.

9. All recordings can be found in your OneDrive.
